# Queue Smart

A healthcare platform designed to support both frontline staff and patients during emergency and urgent care journeys.

Queue Smart helps health workers quickly identify active encounters that need attention, understand service-line flow, and open the right encounter fast. At the same time, it helps patients feel less lost during the care process by giving clearer wait-time updates, next-step guidance, and communication in accessible language.

## What it includes

### Worker side
- CTAS-based encounter prioritization
- Service queue overview
- Patient queue with filtering
- Operational metrics such as average wait time and staff on shift
- Worker-focused workflow for opening and reviewing encounters

### End user side
- Wait-time visibility during the current care journey
- Clear next-step communication
- Simple, controlled updates that reduce uncertainty
- Support for multilingual communication
- Post-experience feedback collection

## What problem it solves

### For health workers
Queue Smart reduces the effort needed to understand who needs attention first, what service lines are under pressure, and which encounter should be opened next.

### For patients
Queue Smart reduces uncertainty during care by helping patients understand what is happening now, what comes next, and how long they may need to wait without exposing unnecessary or overwhelming clinical detail.

## Purpose

The goal of Queue Smart is to create a clear, actionable, and calm interface that supports frontline staff while also improving the patient experience during the current visit. It is not a patient portal and does not aim to expose full patient history on the main dashboard.

## Design principles

- Prioritize encounters by urgency
- Surface salient findings clearly
- Support operational awareness
- Keep the interface scannable and easy to act on
- Reduce uncertainty for patients
- Design for workers first, while protecting patient communication from overload
