# Queue Smart

A worker-facing healthcare dashboard for emergency and urgent care operations.

Queue Smart is designed to help health workers quickly identify active encounters that need attention, understand service-line flow, and open the right encounter fast. The interface combines clinical prioritization, queue visibility, and operational context in one workspace.

## What it includes

- CTAS-based encounter prioritization
- Service queue overview
- Patient queue with filtering
- Operational metrics such as average wait time and staff on shift
- Worker-focused workflow for opening and reviewing encounters

## Purpose

The goal of Queue Smart is to create a clear, actionable, and calm interface that supports frontline staff without overwhelming them. It is not a patient portal and does not aim to expose full patient history on the main dashboard.

## Design principles

- Prioritize encounters by urgency
- Surface salient findings clearly
- Support operational awareness
- Keep the interface scannable and easy to act on
- Design for workers first

## Status

Prototype in progress.

## Notes

This project is focused on concept design and workflow exploration. Some queue and wait-time elements may use mock data to simulate real operational flow.
